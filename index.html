Service what we do

# DOCTORS AT YOUR SERVICE

The aim is to provide the patients with the service and expert advice of the doctors. Patients can have a live chat with the doctors explaining them the symptoms of the disease which they suffer from in order to seek advice from them. Doctors can also fix an appointment, as and when required.

# CHECK DISEASE SPREAD

This project aims to raise awarenes among the msses as well as the government so as to control the widespread of diseases as early as detected, in order to build a healthier and illness-free society.

# CUREMENT OF DISEASE

Patients can seek medical services as well from the pharmacy, under the consultation of the doctor. This will ease the access and services to medical treatment, leading to a sound medical facility, which is the fundamental requirement of the citizns of a developed country.
